Logging In

Open the application.

Enter the username and password provided to you.

Click Login to access your dashboard.

Logging and Viewing Work Hours

Click on Log and View Work Hours.

Select the Date by clicking on the date picker.

Enter the Project Name you are working on.

Provide the Address of your work location:

If working from the office, enter 419 Mt Paul Way.

Enter the Number of Hours Worked.

If you worked at different locations on the same day, make separate entries for each location.

Sign in the blank space before logging hours (entries will not be saved without a signature).

Click Log Hours to submit the entry.

The logged hours will be displayed below in the Information Space.

If you need to edit an entry, contact the admin at admin.hodder@gmail.com.

Note: After logging each entry, go back and refresh the page before making another entry.

Navigating Back

Click Back to Dashboard to return to the main menu.

Filling Out Forms

On the right side of the dashboard, you will find four available forms.

Click on any form to open it.

Fill in all required information.

Click Submit at the bottom of the form to complete the process.

Support

For any issues or technical difficulties, contact the admin at admin.hodder@gmail.com.
